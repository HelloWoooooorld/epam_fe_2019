Grand hostel berlin classic

Tempelhofer Ufer 14 , Berlin, Germany

Hostelworld says

Majestic luxury isn't always easy to come by when you're on a budget, but at the Grand Hostel in Berlin you won't have to feel guilty while you're enjoying its stunning historical setting. The staff here are an added bonus, 'always so friendly' and 'willing to help', you'll feel right at home. The hostel is within walking distance from the Jewish Museum and Checkpoint Charlie, and promise regular pub crawls and guided tours with the staff. Perfect for solo travellers looking to get orientated and make some new friends.

Property Description

HOSCARS 2019: 'Best Hostel in Germany', 'No.1 Hostel in Berlin'.
Moreover Winner Hostel of the German Hostel Contest 'My heavenly Hostel' on VOX TV.
Top location - right in the city's centre. A great yet cheap luxury hostel in the centre of Berlin. Great value accommodation in a central location for travellers to Berlin on a budget.
We serve every morning a large all you can eat breakfast buffet (€ 6.80) and all the things travellers need.
Most of the city's main attractions such as Checkpoint Charlie, the Jewish Museum and the famous Brandenburg Gate are in walking distance.

I had an amazing time staying here! I originally booked it because it was recommended for solo travellers and it didn’t disappoint. The library bar is fantastic for meeting other travellers and they also had socials like the Friday pub crawl and karaoke. There isn’t a kitchen but there’s plenty of lovely food places around. The hostel was close to Mockenbrocke and Glesdreick stations and was really easy to get around sightseeing. This is probably one of the nicest hostels I’ve stayed in!
